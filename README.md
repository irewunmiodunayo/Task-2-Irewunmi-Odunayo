****KEY INSIGHT**

2023 recorded the revenue among all years analyzed.

Among the product category,Chair was the best performing product in terms of revenue generation.

Freeship couponcode was the most effective promotional code

Customers showed strong preference for online payments.

A significant portion of revenue(21.85%) was associated with canceled orders,indicating an area that may require further investigation to reduce potential revenue loss.
